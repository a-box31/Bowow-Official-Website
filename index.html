Bowow

Portfolio

Bowow is a electronic music producer and DJ from New City, New York. The solo creator started in 2016 as a melodic, bass-boosted, EDM, fan in High School going on to study music and sound recording at the University of New Haven.

Inspired by creators such as Avicii, David Guetta, Kygo, and Cash Cash, he created a unique brand of entertainment, fusing melodic, vibing tunes with a variety of fun instrumentals that will get you to be in high spirits. With an award winning portfolio and a bright future, he continues to hone his music production. He uses industry standard music DAWs, synthesizes new sounds, and is in touch with up and coming artists to create a unique feel for his music. Always bringing his talent to the table, he is welcoming singers, rappers, and the like to work with him. Knowing the best songs are a combination of the music, voice, and message, he brings the best out of the songs he is on. Coming soon is his new single. For the foreseeing future, he plans to release a series of singles. Each calling upon aspects of good music. Up for a challenge? He can throw down in beat box battles or collaborate with rappers to bring the best out of everyone. This is the core of his music. To call on the musical spirit of the the crowd. He looks forward to his career DJing this kind of music. Remember his name.

Bowow
   Inspired by creators such as Avicii, David Guetta, Kygo, and Cash Cash, he created a unique brand of entertainment, fusing melodic, vibing tunes with a variety of fun instrumentals that will get you to be in high spirits. With an award winning portfolio and a bright future, he continues to hone his music production. He uses industry standard music DAWs, synthesizes new sounds, and is in touch with up and coming artists to create a unique feel for his music.

   Bowow is a electronic music producer and DJ from New City, New York. The solo creator started in 2016 as a melodic, bass-boosted, EDM, fan going on to study music and sound recording at the University of New Haven. Bowow's passion for music and dedication to honing his craft led him to create captivating and high-energy electronic tracks that resonate with audiences.
